About Us

Gizra is known amongst its clients as efficient executors of complex web projects. Established in 2009 , we employ a team of developers of wide and diverse code-writing capabilities and focus on Open Source technologies, specifically Drupal.

Branded as a leading web developer, Gizra has contributed modules which have been adopted by developers worldwide. Our different modules and code contributions are used in leading sites throughout the world.

Harvard University, the United Nations, the European Commission are some of our clients.
