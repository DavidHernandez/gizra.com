What is Openscholar
OpenScholar is a full featured solution for academic websites, used by major institutions including Harvard University, Princeton, Berkeley and others. A fully open source Drupal-based software, OpenScholar was created to allow non-technical users to easily build and manage websites from personal to department levels. Gizra is among the lead developers of OpenScholar, along with leading universities worldwide.
