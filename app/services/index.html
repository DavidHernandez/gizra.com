Services We Partner On

Open Source and Drupal CMS Development

From its establishment, Gizra has focused on Open Source development, in particular in the Drupal CMS field where it is responsible for developing key modules for Drupal 7 and Drupal 8 among them Organic Groups, Message Stack, Entity Reference, RESTful which has been adopted by developers worldwide as performance enhancers and are actively maintained by Gizra personnel. Gizra is a member of the Drupal Association and our team consists of known Drupal core developers.

Strategy and Consulting

A website isn’t simply a product - it’s the application of a solution, and to get to that application requires a strategy. Gizra employs a lean, yet comprehensive, discovery process that puts the client’s needs first, seeks to understand the user stories, and quickly determines the appropriate tools for the job. Our strategic development process comes from years of experience in finding simple human solutions to complex technical challenges.

User Experience and Design

We are a technology company, but people are at the center of our universe. We definitely get excited about the tools that we use, but they all serve a singular purpose: to engage a user in an experience with your content. That experience can take many forms - it may be very complicated, or quite simple, but at the end of the day, when the last piece of code is committed, we should be left with an uncomplicated relationship between your users and your website.
