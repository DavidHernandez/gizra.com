Strategy & Consulting

A modern website is the application of a solution, and to get to that application requires a strategy. Gizra employs a lean, yet comprehensive, discovery process that puts the client’s needs first, seeks to understand the users, and determine the appropriate tools.

The way we partner with you should also be exact and therefore our strategy and consulting models are designed to specifically fit your company’s digital needs.

Project Strategy

At the discovery phase, we partner with you to clarify your objectives and determine the best tools and way to get there. At the end of the discovery phase, you will have a clear vision and a detailed task and budget breakdown. We can also step into a project at different points of the journey, from start to finish or at a specific point that requires some extra attention.

Digital Strategy

Your organization is really good at what they do and require the right technology strategy to support it. Depending on your organization’s requirements, this would occur with close partnership with your CTO or fill the position if required.

Audit

A swift and exacting process to identify issues along with a plan to fix them (with or without us.) This is a great option to get insight to your digital strategy status and to prioritize and remedy technical issues.

IT Staff Augmentation

Add top notch Drupal architects to your team while skipping the steep learning curve and maintaining the flexibility to ramp-up or down your staff to match your business needs. We also offer small team training sessions.
