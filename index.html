About

Gizra is a boutique Drupal studio. We build tailored web sites and advanced web applications using Drupal CMS, Node.js, Jekyll and other technologies. We specialize in creating Drupal modules, in particular in the social and education space, and have extensive experience working with multinational teams employing an agile, continuous deployment process.

Gizra team created and maintains key Drupal modules such as Organic Groups, Message and Entity Reference, and contributes to Drupal core. We're deeply involved in the Drupal community and speak regularly at Drupal conference worldwide.
